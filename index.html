24/7 DDos Mitigation

Fastly offers on-demand distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attack mitigation, protecting your business from the most common attack vectors through a simple, cost effective model. Because we’re a large, multi-terabit-per-second, globally distributed network, we can withstand sustained and significant attacks against our network — a benefit which we pass along to our customers.

  • Respond to NTP and DNS amplification / reflection attacks and complex layer 7 DNS or HTTP/HTTPS-based attacks

    • Harmful Effects

    Lost revenue.

    Organizations that need to be online for their business (such as ecommerce or gaming)  have the most to lose, with an average daily revenue loss of $2M. 1

    Increased costs.

    Even a single attack can cost your company up to $450K.2

    Customer Attrition

    If your users are unable to access your website — during a DDoS attack or otherwise — chances are they won’t come back. Users aren’t willing to wait; 40% of people abandon a website that takes longer than three seconds to load. 3
